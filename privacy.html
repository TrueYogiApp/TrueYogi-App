Privacy Policy

Last Updated: Feb 1st 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

1. Information We Collect

  • Wallet Data: Public wallet addresses to award tokens.
  • Session Data: Meditation session times and completion records.
  • Device Data: Device type (mobile/desktop) and IP address for security and analytics.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide and maintain the app.
  • Record meditation sessions and award YOGI tokens.
  • Use device data to protect against abuse, improve features and fix bugs.
  • Communicate important updates.

3. Sharing of Data

  • Blockchain networks for token transactions.
  • Service providers to run our backend.
  • Law enforcement if legally required.
  • We never sell your personal data.

4. Data Retention

We keep your data only as long as necessary to provide the service, or as required by law.

5. Security

We use encryption, secure servers, and best practices to protect your data. However, no online service is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

  • Access the data we hold about you.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Opt out of certain data uses.

(Rights may vary depending on your location.)

7. International Users

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or California, you have additional rights under GDPR/CCPA regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, delete, or transfer your data.

8. Analytics & Cookies
